Our Story

Two Decades of Excellence

Founded in 2005, ZIS Fitouts has grown from a specialist fitout contractor into a comprehensive construction and interior services company covering the full spectrum of fitout, fabrication, structural, joinery, renovation, and landscaping.

Over the past 20 years we have built a reputation for delivering projects that exceed expectations — on time, on budget, and built to last. Our clients range from multinational corporations to private villa owners, and we bring the same commitment to quality to every single one.

We are passionate about creating spaces where people feel inspired. Every project is approached with fresh eyes, deep listening, and a commitment to understanding what truly matters to our clients.
