Renovation Works

04

Reviving Spaces. Restoring Value.

Whether it's a tired apartment in need of a refresh or a commercial premises requiring a full strip-out and refit, our renovation team brings the expertise to transform existing spaces efficiently and beautifully.

We manage every aspect of the renovation process — from demolition and civil works through to final finishes — minimizing disruption and delivering on time and on budget.

What We Do

Complete Renovation Solutions

01
Demolition & Strip-Out
Safe and efficient demolition, hacking, strip-out of existing finishes, partitions, ceilings, and floors — with proper disposal and site clearance.
02
Civil & Masonry Works
Blockwork, brickwork, concrete repairs, structural openings, lintel installations, waterproofing, and all civil construction works.
03
Plastering & Rendering
Sand & cement plastering, gypsum skim coat, external render, decorative finishes, and wall levelling to a premium standard.
04
Painting Works
Interior and exterior painting using premium brands — including feature walls, texture finishes, stucco veneziano, specialist coatings, and anti-mould treatments.
05
MEP Upgrades
Electrical rewiring, lighting upgrades, plumbing modifications, AC replacements, and all MEP upgrade works as part of a full renovation scope.
06
Waterproofing
Bathroom and wet area waterproofing, roof waterproofing, basement tanking, and remedial waterproofing treatments for leaks and dampness.
07
Kitchen Renovation
Full kitchen refurbishments including new layouts, tiling, cabinet replacement, countertop upgrades, appliance fitting, and splashback works.
08
Bathroom Renovation
Complete bathroom makeovers — new tiling, sanitary ware, shower upgrades, vanity units, mirrors, accessories, and plumbing connections.
09
External Facade Works
External plastering, painting, cladding, sealant replacement, facade cleaning, and all external building envelope renovation works.
