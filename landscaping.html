Landscaping

05

Outdoor Spaces as Beautiful as Your Interior

Our landscaping team creates outdoor environments that are both stunning and sustainable in the UAE climate. From intimate villa gardens to expansive commercial landscapes, we combine planting expertise with architectural hardscaping to craft outdoor spaces that delight year-round.

We design, supply, install, and maintain — offering a complete end-to-end landscaping service across Dubai and the UAE.

What We Do

Outdoor Landscape Creation

01
Landscape Design
Concept design, planting plans, 3D visualisations, and full landscape masterplanning for residential and commercial outdoor spaces.
02
Planting & Soft Landscaping
Supply and installation of trees, palms, shrubs, ground covers, grasses, and seasonal plants — selected for the UAE climate and client aesthetic.
03
Irrigation Systems
Automated drip and spray irrigation systems, timers, soil moisture sensors, and water-efficient solutions to keep landscapes thriving.
04
Hardscaping
Paving, stone pathways, decking, retaining walls, steps, raised planters, and all hard landscape elements in stone, concrete, or timber.

Swimming Pool Surrounds

High-quality artificial turf installation for gardens, rooftops, balconies, and play areas — low-maintenance and UV-resistant for UAE conditions.
07
Water Features
Custom fountains, cascades, reflecting pools, wall-mounted water features, and decorative ponds that bring movement and serenity to outdoor spaces.
08
Outdoor Lighting

09

Ongoing maintenance contracts covering trimming, fertilising, pest control, irrigation servicing, and seasonal planting refreshes.
